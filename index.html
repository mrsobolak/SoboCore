About Sobolak Technologies

Sobolak Technologies is an independent technology company based in Michigan, United States. We build software and media platforms for people who value privacy, transparency, and real information.

We started as a local-first software company — building AI tools that run entirely on your own hardware with no cloud dependency. That chapter has closed. Our focus has shifted to media.

Our current flagship is LiveLeak.site — a full community-run revival of the original LiveLeak platform, rebuilt from scratch and self-hosted on our own servers. No corporate backing. No investor pressure. No censorship agenda.
