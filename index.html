WHAT IS SoOS?

An Operating System Made By No One

SoOS (pronounced "so-oh-ess") is a free, lightweight operating system built on a custom Linux kernel. It was designed to be simple, fast, and fun — with everything you need and nothing you don't.

SoOS comes pre-installed with a web browser, file manager, text editor, media player, chat client, email, and more. Plus our exclusive desktop companion: CubeyPET! NEW!

SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS

Minimum

  • Pentium III 500 MHz or equivalent
  • 256 MB RAM
  • 2 GB hard drive space
  • VGA display (800x600)
  • CD-ROM drive

Recommended

  • Intel Core 2 Duo or better
  • 1 GB RAM or more
  • 10 GB hard drive space
  • 1024x768 display or higher
  • Sound card (for CubeyPET!)

SoOS is compatible with most x86 hardware. Tested on Dell, HP, Lenovo, and custom builds.
