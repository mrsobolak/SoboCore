About Sobolak Technologies

Sobolak Technologies is an independent technology company based in Michigan, United States. We've been through a few chapters so far.

We started as a local-first software company, building AI tools that ran entirely on your own hardware with no cloud dependency. That chapter closed in early 2026 when our focus shifted to media — first with LiveLeak.site, a from-scratch community revival of the original LiveLeak, and later with SoboVids.com, which replaced it as our flagship video platform. SoboVids.com was shut down in late summer 2026.

Right now we're between chapters. AI development is back on the roadmap, and we're opening a new storefront: computers, from the 90s through the 2020s. See what's coming below.
