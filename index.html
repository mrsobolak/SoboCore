FAQ

Fast answers that build trust.

Does SoboAI need internet?
Only for the first model download. After that, you can run offline.
What hardware do I need?
A modern CPU works fine. A GPU helps a lot for faster responses. More RAM lets you run larger models.
Can I export my chats?
Yes. Export to TXT or Markdown.
Is my data stored online?
No. The goal is to keep your data local to your machine.
